Terracare Associates is an outdoor maintenance company that you can count on; a company that delivers superior and reliable service at the best value. For more than 25 years, TCA has adhered to the highest standards in the business for customer satisfaction.

Best Value and Superior Service Quality - We understand everyone is on a budget. We too strive to run our business profitably without sacrificing quality. Our teams are sensitive to your budget needs and can build flexible programs designed to manage costs while still providing the value you expect. Our customizable maintenance contracts allow our customers the flexibility to adapt to their ever changing budgets. We are absolutely committed to helping our customers make the most of their budgets and spend their money wisely.

Communication:

Our Account and Project Managers are the eyes and ears of your property; they are trained to provide reliable and responsive communication. Whether you need customized reporting or a weekly, monthly or quarterly site walk, TCA is eager to provide you with a unique experience. In addition, our field managers have email, text and radio access 24/7.

Customer Service level objectives

TCA knows reliable and effective communication is of upmost importance for you to achieve absolute customer confidence in your landscape maintenance provider. We have set internal goals and incentives to ensure prompt response times, as well as dependable and consistent communication.

Experience:

Terracare Associates understands that having a highly trained staff that is able to develop a flexible and efficient plan to manage your properties is key to successfully serving our clients. Our team members are certified, experienced, dedicated and professional.

Safety:

Safety is about people. Keeping our employees safe and healthy everyday is priority one. Every employee attends extensive safety training before working in the field with our company and mandatory weekly tailgate meetings to review specific safety practice reviews. The staff also reviews job safety record and proactively mitigates risk through open and honest discussion of improvement. We offer incentives to those that continue to use excellent safety practices.

Responsibility/Sustainability

As an organization, our passion is to give back to the communities where we live, work and play while being good stewards of our local environment protecting the health, safety and welfare of our communities, clients and staff. See Sustainability and Responsibility Policy
