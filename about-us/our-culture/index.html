“Terracare Associates-TCA is like a family”
~TCA Team member

Terracare Associates-TCA is committed to excellence. In a recent employee survey 96% said, “I am proud to work for Terracare Associates-TCA every day.”

Culture goes beyond business strategy - It is the foundation of our company.

We LIVE this excellence every day.

  • Through Leadership we only hire qualified people for all positions and then empower them so they can lead by example.

  • Through Integrity we make decisions based on what is right, not just based on looking for the easy solution and we always strive to work hard, show up on time, and do what we say we are going to do.

  • We Value training and education and are focused on providing a safe and customer service oriented work environment.

  • We are committed to the Environment we work and live in through our commitment to volunteering in our communities and our passion for the preservation of resources for future generations.

Employee Engagement

We know that our team members are our best asset. With very low turnover of our full time employees, we bring you the reliability of consistent and dependable service year-after-year.

We do this by encouraging team members to give L.I.V.E. awards to colleagues who are doing an excellent job. We continually pride ourselves by offering employees a safe culture of communication with management to talk one-on-one about all aspects of the job. We reward our team members on a regular basis through employee picnics and special days at the office to encourage a sense comradery and community.
