Company Profile:

Terracare Associates (TCA) is a leading outdoor maintenance company in the Western United States with almost 30 years of landscape management and infrastructure maintenance experience in assisting both public and commercial needs. Terracare Associates is headquartered in Littleton, Colorado and maintains operations in the California Bay area, Greater Salt Lake City, Utah and Denver metropolitan areas.

Our markets include:
  • Public Landscape Services
  • Public Infrastructure Maintenance
  • Commercial Landscape Services

Our company began in 1985. As a single crew providing landscape maintenance services TCA has grown into a company of over 400 employees that serves hundreds of diverse clients with multiple properties. Each day, Terracare Associates team members strive to exceed industry standards and build long term partnerships with our clients. Our goal is always to achieve absolute customer confidence. During all seasons, our certified and experienced staff help to maintain and improve landscapes, parks, roadways and infrastructure for a beautiful, healthy, safe and sustainable outdoor environments.
