Our key purpose is to build a lasting partnership with our customers. We know Customer Service is your number one concern when looking for a public and commercial landscape company, and we take this very seriously.

We are committed to:
  • Trust

    We don’t just want satisfied customers; we want customers who love working with us.  Building lifelong relationships is key to our health and well-being.

  • Communication

    When you choose Terracare Associates-TCA as your landscape company, you benefit from the most well-trained crews and professional technicians in the field. You are assigned an English speaking Account or Project Manager whose sole job is to oversee all landscape maintenance or public works maintenance for your property. Account Managers work with crew members who are the eyes and ears of your property. Each Account Manager is specifically trained to answer to your needs - providing reliable and responsive communication, making sure you are absolutely satisfied. Whether you need customized month-end reporting or a weekly site walk, Terracare Associates-TCA has it covered.

  • Quality Assurance Program

    Our project manager, account manager, foremen and upper management conduct regularly scheduled quality control audits. This allows us to generate work orders for any necessary repairs in our work order management system and complete the necessary corrections as soon as we can.

  • Team approach

    Our entire business is built around the concept of “team”. We manage through empowering our teams to give the best quality possible. Your personalized service is paramount because our team is allowed to rely on internal expertise, equipment and resources to meet your deadlines in the most efficient and effective way possible.
