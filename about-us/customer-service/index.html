Our goal is to build a long term partnership with our customers. We know Customer Service is your number one attribute when looking for a outdoor maintenance provider.

We are committed to:
  • Trust

    When it comes to your outdoor maintenance, you need a company you can count on; a company that delivers superior and reliable service at the best value. For almost three decades, Terracare Associates has adhered to the highest standards in the business for customer satisfaction. Our customers are our most important asset. We don’t just want satisfied customers; we want customers who love working with us. Building lifelong relationships with our customers is our ultimate goal.   • Communication

    When you choose TCA as your landscape maintenance provider, not only do you receive the attention of our well-trained crews and professional technicians, you are assigned an Account Manager whose job is to oversee all landscape and irrigation care for your property. Our Account Managers work with the crew members who are the eyes and ears of your property. Each Account Manager is trained to provide reliable and responsive communication making sure you’re absolutely happy with the service we are providing. Whether you need customized month- end reporting or a weekly site walk, Terracare Associates is eager to provide you with a unique experience.

  • Quality Assurance Program

    Our project manager, account manager, foremen and upper management conduct regularly scheduled quality control audits. We then generate work orders for any necessary repairs in our work order management system and complete the necessary corrections.

  • Team approach

    We have an established culture that is focused on listening and responding quickly to the needs of the customers through deploying a team to each job. Our entire business is built around the concept of “team”. This gives you personalized service while allowing TCA’s team easy access to internal expertise, equipment and resources to complete projects in a timely and efficient manner.
