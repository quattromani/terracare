Terracare Associates (TCA), is one of the top 50 commercial landscape maintenance companies in the USA.

It is our passion to help your landscape reach its maximum potential, increasing your property value while providing an environment for your business or community to grow. Our experienced staff of highly trained professionals will personally assist you to clarify your landscape objectives and determine your outdoor maintenance needs. We offer a wide array of services including complete landscape management and renovation, irrigation system maintenance, lake and pond management, annual color installations, plant health care, erosion control, wetland and drought mitigation and snow removal. We have the resources of a large landscape company while providing a personalized crew that is dedicated to your property.

Here is what we provide:

  • Value: Lowering Your Cost Through Efficiency - Terracare Associates’ field crews utilize CREWtek™, a handheld device that lets our staff manage projects remotely, handle billing, manage equipment and schedule crews efficiently and effectively. We know when to deploy the right equipments and people to do the job in a timely performance with technical competence. All TCA vehicles are equipped with the latest GPS equipment. This technology allows us to track time spent at your site and provides a method for efficient routing, especially in emergencies, so we can be at your site at the right time.
  • Customer Service level objectives – TCA knows reliable and effective communication is of upmost importance for you to achieve absolute customer confidence in your landscape maintenance provider. We have set internal goals to ensure prompt response times, as well as dependable and consistent communication.
  • Quality Assurance Program – Our Account Managers, Supervisors, Crew, Foremen and upper management conduct quality control audits throughout the year. We then generate necessary work orders for repairs or maintenance solutions in our work order management system. Corrections are then completed in a timely and efficient manner.
  • Experienced Project Team - At Terracare Associates we have an established culture that is focused on listening and responding quickly to the needs of our customer. Our entire business is built around the “team” concept. To begin a project with this focus is critical. It is to our customers advantage that we can provide resources of a large company while providing the personal service of a ‘mom and pop’ landscape company. See Effective Teams blog
  • Safety - is a core value at Terracare Associates. Every employee attends safety training before working in the field with our company and mandatory weekly tailgate meetings to review specific safety practices reviews. The staff also reviews job safety record and proactively mitigates risk through open and honest discussion of improvement. We offer incentives to those that continue to use excellent safety practices.
  • Plant Health Care - Our team of professionals will bring out the best in your landscape using a strict Integrated Pest Management (IPM) schedule that focuses on properly feeding your landscape while monitoring the plant material and using pesticides only as a last resort to control an insect, fungus or weed problem.
  • Innovative Approach to Landscape - Staying on top of emerging trends and embracing new technologies enables Terracare Associates to offer solutions that are innovative and unique. We offer seminars on a variety of topics to help educate our clients on best landscape practices for your properties.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible to the environment and our citizens is part of who we are, and what we strive for, in our everyday work. See Sustainability & Responsibility Policy
