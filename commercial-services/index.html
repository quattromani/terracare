Commercial Landscape Maintenance

Terracare Associates-TCA is one of the top 50 commercial landscape maintenance companies in the USA.

Our mission is to ensure your landscape reaches its maximum potential. The true benefit to you – an increase in property value and a safe and healthy environmental that partners well within a growing business and community. Our experienced staff of highly trained professionals will personally assist you in meeting your landscape goals and outdoor maintenance needs.

We offer a wide array of services:
  • Complete landscape management and renovation
  • Irrigation system maintenance
  • Lake and pond management
  • Annual color installations
  • Plant health care
  • Erosion control
  • Wetland and drought mitigation
  • Snow removal

We bring you the breadth of resources that a large landscape company has to offer with a very personalized approach. Our team members are dedicated to your property.

You will benefit from:
  • Value: Lowering Your Costs - Field crews understand the latest technologies - such as CREWtek™ - to deploy the best suited equipment and team members dedicated to your personal needs. 
  • Quality Assurance Program – Account Managers and an experienced management team conduct quality control audits throughout the year to ensure we are meeting the best quality standards.
  • Experienced Project Team - Terracare Associates-TCA has a proven track record in listening and responding quickly to the needs of our customers and proactively seeking out solutions to best serve your needs.
  • Safety - Safety is of the upmost importance to Terracare Associates-TCA and a core value.  Our goal is for every employee, and all of our customers to go home safe and healthy every day.
  • Plant Health Care - Our team of professionals will bring out the best in your landscape using a strict Integrated Pest Management (IPM) practices. We have a certified arborist on staff to bring expert advice for your plants, tree and turf.
  • Leaders in Landscape - Our team members are continually sought out for landscape expertise.  We offer seminars on a variety of topics to help educate our community leaders and customers on the best landscape practices in the Industry.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible to the environment and our community is part of our core values; we strive to be good corporate citizens every day and on every job.
