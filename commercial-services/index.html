Terracare Associates (TCA), is one of the top 50 commercial landscape maintenance companies in the USA. It is our passion to help your landscape reach its maximum potential, increasing your property value while providing an environment for your business or community to grow. Our experienced staff of highly trained professionals will personally assist you to clarify your landscape objectives and determine your outdoor maintenance needs. We offer a wide array of services including complete landscape management and renovation, irrigation system maintenance, lake and pond management, annual color installations, plant health care, erosion control, wetland and drought mitigation and snow removal. We have the resources of a large landscape company while providing a personalized crew that is dedicated to your property.

Here is what we provide:

  • Value: Lowering Your Cost Through Efficiency - Terracare Associates’ field crews utilizes latest technologies such as CREWtek™, to deploy the right equipments and people to do the job efficiently and effectively.
  • Customer Service level objectives – Your Account Managers and management team conducts quality control audits throughout the year to ensure quality service.
  • Quality Assurance Program – Our Account Managers, Supervisors, Crew, Foremen and upper management conduct quality control audits throughout the year. We then generate necessary work orders for repairs or maintenance solutions in our work order management system. Corrections are then completed in a timely and efficient manner.
  • Experienced Project Team - At Terracare Associates we have an established culture promoting “teams”. This approach is focused on listening and responding quickly to the needs of our customer while proactively finding solutions and best serve your property.
  • Safety - is a core value at Terracare Associates. Our goal is for every employee, and all of our customers to go home safe and healthy every day.
  • Plant Health Care - Our team of professionals will bring out the best in your landscape using a strict Integrated Pest Management (IPM) practices.
  • Leaders in Landscape - Our team members are asked to present as industry experts in landscape. We offer seminars on a variety of topics to help educate our clients on best landscape practices for your properties.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible to the environment and our community is part of our core values; we strive to be good corporate citizens every day and on every job.
