With more than 20 years in the lake management industry, Terracare Associates has the expertise to maximize the aesthetic value of your lakes and water features. Our experienced staff, including an aquatic biologist, can provide complete maintenance and renovation services.

Water Quality Maintenance

Provide expert analysis of existing water quality conditions in your lake or pond, and develop specific recommendations to maintain and/or improve the water quality in your aquatic system.

Vegetation and Algae Control

Develop a lake maintenance program integrating physical, mechanical and/or chemical control methods to balance aquatic plant and algae populations in aquatic systems. Site specific use of herbicides and algaecides as a management tool for the control of nuisance aquatic plant and algae species.

Mechanical Systems Maintenance

Fountain and aeration system maintenance to improve lake aesthetics, water movement and oxygen deficiency concerns.

Aquatic Harvesting

Aquatic weed control utilizing aquatic plant harvesters to cut and remove aquatic weed growth from lakes and other waterways.

Lake Plan Development

Consulting and drafting of LMP’s associated with the development, construction and maintenance of lake systems.

Lake Construction

Terracare Associates can also provide lake construction services including excavation, liner installation, bulkhead wall repairs, aeration systems install and aquatic vegetation planting.

Our Expert Stuart Perry – Aquatic Service Manager

Mr. Perry has over twenty eight years experience in research and specialized aquatic vegetation control programs utilized in aquatic management. Prior to joining Terracare Associates, Mr Perry performed extensive aquatic research at the university level in addition to owning and operating a lake management firm for fifteen years. Since Terracare Associates’ acquisition of Mr. Perry’s company, he has served as the Aquatic Service Manager for Terracare Associates. Stuart specializes in developing site specific lake management plans, water quality monitoring, compliance and control programs. He is very involved in the implementation of best management practices in waterways as well as providing exemplary customer services to our lake management clients.
