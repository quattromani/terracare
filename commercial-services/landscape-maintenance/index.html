Inside the Top 100: Terracare Associates with Dean Murphy

Dean Murphy shares his insight on how Terracare Associates operates, how the company has grown to this size, and how he and his team manage it. It’s an exclusive opportunity for L&L readers look behind the scenes at how one of the largest companies in the landscape and lawn care industry runs.

As a commercial landscape company, our commitment is to deliver timely performance with technical competence on every property we service. We pride ourselves in delivering personalized service to every client through a dedicated team approach. We have a wide breadth of commercial properties including retail, corporate offices, institutions and commercial properties and many clients we have serviced more than 5 years. TCA currently manages over 95 Home Owners Associations (HOA’s), neighborhood properties and have built lasting relationships with both property manager and the HOA board of directors as a trusted partner.

Our team of landscape management professionals is experienced, dedicated and our team will partner with you to ensure a beautiful, healthy, safe and sustainable outdoor environment.
