Commercial Landscape Maintenance

Terracare Associates-TCA manages more than 750 commercial landscape jobs across three states and employs the latest technology, irrigation and plant health care specialists. You will receive superior and dependable service year round.

Lawn & Landscape magazine Top 100 List ranked Terracare Associates-TCA at #39 top landscape companies in the United States.

Commercial Properties

As a commercial landscape company, our commitment is to deliver timely performance with technical competence on every property we serve. We pride ourselves in delivering personalized service to every client through a dedicated team approach.  We have a wide breadth of commercial properties including retail, corporate campuses, institutions and multi-family complexes and offices have continued to serve many existing clients year after year.

Homeowners Association (HOA)

Terracare Associates-TCA currently manages the maintenance of more than 95 Homeowner Association (HOA) neighborhood properties. We have built lasting relationships with both property managers and the HOA board of directors to serve these neighborhoods very successfully.

Our team of landscape management professionals is experienced and dedicated and our team will partner with you to ensure a beautiful, healthy, safe and sustainable outdoor environment.
