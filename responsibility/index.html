Innovation is a key component to the Terracare Associates Environmental Restoration group’s success. Using advanced installation techniques, smarter material applications and custom designed equipment; Terracare Associates is changing the environmental restoration industry. Our well-trained crews perform reclamation, hydroseeding, erosion control and wetlands services for many clients each year.

Our commitment to improving and restoring our environments natural processes has been a driving force in our success. Through a diverse and knowledgeable staff we are able to share our success with our clients.

State agencies and other municipalities, private developers, and homeowners associations are just a few of the clients who trust Terracare Associates to perform diverse reclamation services on challenging sites.

Our highly mobile teams of professionals understand the environmental impact represented by each and every reclamation project. We take our responsibility seriously and consistently consult with our clients and other design professionals to construct and maintain our projects efficiently, accurately and cost effectively. We work with our clients as well as regulatory agencies like the E.P.A, Army Corp of Engineers, state health departments and other stakeholders with an interest in the environmental impact of construction and water quality. Terracare Associates Environmental Restoration consistently provides cost-effective construction solutions to difficult and demanding site challenges.

Terracare Associates Environmental Restoration team is dedicated to the improvement and preservation of our environment through the application of “best management practices” in every phase of reclamation, hydroseeding, erosion control and wetlands management.
