Public Private Partnerships

Public landscape and Public Infrastructure Services

For almost three decades, Terracare Associates (TCA) has been at the forefront of outdoor maintenance including public-private partnership (P3). TCA specializes in public landscape management and public Infrastructure maintenance services. At TCA we strive for superior customer service. We are a trusted partner, utilize the latest technologies and communicate effectively to ensure absolute customer confidence. Experience matters, with a leadership team average experience is 15+ years in the industry. Our highly trained staff is flexible and efficient in managing the maintenance of physical infrastructure, parks, streetscapes and roadways to successfully serve cities, counties public highway authorities and metro districts.

Terracare Associates can provide you:
  • An Experienced Project Team - We have an established culture that is focused on listening to and responding quickly to the needs of the customer. Our entire business is built around the concept of “team”. To begin a project with this focus is a critical advantage.
  • Quality Assurance Program – Our Project Manager, Assistant Project Manager, Foremen and upper management will conduct quality control audits. We will then generate any necessary work orders for repair or maintenance solutions in our work order management system and complete the necessary corrections.
  • Complete Scope of Services to Meet Your Needs - Terracare Associates offers a single-source contact for your land management needs, providing services including but not limited to: native mowing, weed control, landscape maintenance, public works, road repair, signage, snow and storm water management.
  • Honor Schedules and Budgets - We understand the importance of keeping projects on schedule and on budget while maintaining the level of service you desire. Our team members are able to respond quickly when called upon for your outdoor maintenance needs, and we work closely with multi-governmental agencies.
  • Strong Governmental Experience - The Terracare Associates leadership and management have extensive experience working with a number of governmental entities. We are familiar with managing public expectations and incorporating effective communication processes for a wide variety of stakeholders.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible is part of our everyday work.
