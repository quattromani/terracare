  • Terracare’s Community Gardens, 110 Magazine
    Terracare Associates Vice President Bill Horn and staff, has organized, built and maintained several edible gardens at five local schools. The most recent school involved coordination with the faculty and students at O’Hara park middle school in Oakely, California to create a learning garden for students and teachers to use throughout the year.
  • Ty Wilson, Achieves Landscape Industry certified status, Sacramento Bee
    PLANET, the national landscape industry trade association, announces that Ty Wilson, Branch Manager at Terracare Associates in Elk Grove, CA has earned the Landscape Industry Certified Technician designation and is now nationally certified. The achievement of the Landscape Industry Certified designation demonstrates a distinguished level of professionalism. Only a select few, approximately 1% of landscape professionals working in the United States today, are certified
  • People on the Move – Dean Murphy, Denver Business Journal
