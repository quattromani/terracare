One of the largest public-private partnerships of its kind in the U.S. Terracare Associates operates as the city public works department for the city of Centennial in Colorado. After successfully completing a five year contract, the Centennial’s city council voted to extend its contract and TCA will continue provide public works services for five more year for its 103,000 residents. The partnership with CH2M Hill and the city has garnered numerous awards, including the 2010 National Council for Public-Private Partnerships Service Award; 2009 American Public Works Association-Colorado Chapter Innovative Customer Service Call Center Award; 2010 APWA Innovative Customer Service Call Center Award; and 2011 APWA-Colorado Maintenance-Operation for Energy Award.

TCA is also attuned to the inherent difficulties of public expectation and prioritizes effective communication with a wide variety of stakeholders. We offer a high degree of professionalism that assists our public works clients in the timely completion of critical infrastructure projects.

We understand having a highly trained staff that is able to develop a flexible and efficient plan to manage the maintenance of your physical outdoor the infrastructure and communicate needs is key to outstanding client relations. The TCA teams for our public infrastructure maintenance projects are led by experienced, highly trained, professional Project Managers who will have responsibility for the entire project.

With 400 employees nationwide, TCA has the experience, knowledge and ability to provide quality public works/infrastructure maintenance services to our clients. Our teams are experienced, dedicated and professional.
