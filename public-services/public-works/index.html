One of the largest public-private partnerships of its kind in the U.S., Terracare Associates-TCA operates as the city public works department for the city of Centennial in Colorado (serving 103,000 residents).

After successfully completing a five year contract, the Centennial’s city council voted to extend its contract and Terracare Associates-TCA will continue to provide public works services for five more years.

Our partnership with the City of Centennial and private industry leader CH2M Hill makes this project so successful that it has garnered numerous awards including: National Council for Public-Private Partnerships Service Award, American Public Works Association-Colorado Chapter Innovative Customer Service Call Center Award, APWA Innovative Customer Service Call Center Award, and APWA-Colorado Maintenance-Operation for Energy Award.

Expertise in the public-private partnership landscape gives us a full understanding of the inherent difficulties of public expectation. We are proactive in developing communication plan that works for each government agency as well as a wide range of stakeholders. We offer a high degree of professionalism and this allows us to continually deliver the timely completion of critical infrastructure projects and meet the highest standards of our public works clients.

Terracare Associates-TCA also serves as public works and/or maintenance contractor for the City of Lone Tree, CO, Cottonwood Heights, Utah and numerous districts and authorities throughout the region.

Public Works Service may include:
  • Striping
  • Asphalt – pot holes and patching
  • Concrete
  • Signs
  • Sweeping
  • Plowing – snow and ice
  • Emergency response
  • Storm management/drainage
  • Landscape
  • Native mowing
  • Lighting
  • Road maintenance
  • Crack Sealing
  • Shoulder maintenance
  • Pedestrian light (street crossing)
  • Traffic control
  • Dead animal removal
