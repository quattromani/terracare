March, 2014 - Terracare Associates, headquartered in Littleton, Colorado, is pleased to announce the recent acquisition of all assets of All American Barricade Company out of Sandy, Utah. TCA has appointed Mr. Loran Gillen, past owner of All American Barricade, as the Manager of Terracare Associates Utah operations. Mr. Gillen has 29 years of experience in the industry with ten different specialty certifications. All American Barricade has provided professional traffic control and safety services since 2009. Terracare Associates will utilize our skilled, experienced, and state ATSSA certified staff to offer a new service line assisting with all traffic control needs.

“This was a great move for my company after 29 years in the industry, because road maintenance safety is of up most importance. Controlling traffic during events, construction and repairs is a critical piece for minimal impact on traffic flow and keeping the public healthy and safe.”
