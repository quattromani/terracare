Since streetscapes within the city begin to define the surrounding uses and social environment, Terracare Associates with their extensive horticulture knowledge have been able to create lasting streetscapes that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but diverse of plant material, texture and color.

Terracare Associates, along with the guidelines for urban development, have forged successful partnerships. Using best management practices and effective communication we have also solidified a productive relationship with the urban community. Terracare Associates commitment to our local community is showcased by the success of the urban landscapes we maintain. Since the typical urban environment is made up of several integral parts operating as a whole, governmental facilities, performing arts complexes, financial institutions, restaurants and housing; our successful management of each these individual parts ensures success.
