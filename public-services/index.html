Terracare Associates, LLC is a recognized leader in the public outdoor maintenance arena of municipal landscape maintenance, public works contracting and public-private partnerships (P3).

We specialize in city landscape management and public infrastructure maintenance services. We strive for superior customer service and are a trusted partner with dedicated and professional staff, state-of-the-art technologies, and reliable equipment to get the job done. Effective communication with our customers is of upmost importance and this gives us the highest of ratings in customer confidence.

Experience matters. Our leadership team has an average of more than 20 years of experience in outdoor maintenance, landscape and construction industries. We bring you a wide range of experience and man power with 500 employees nationwide. Employees are cross-trained in multiple service lines to maximize your cost efficiency. Highly trained staff are also flexible in managing and maintaining physical infrastructure, parks, streetscapes, highways and roadways to successfully serve cities, counties, public highway authorities and metro districts.

Terracare Associates can provide you with:
  • Strong Governmental Experience - Public-private partnership (P3) is a way for governments to collaborate with the private sector to share the risks and rewards involved in the construction and maintenance of public infrastructure projects.  Terracare Associates-TCA leadership has extensive experience working with P3 projects and a number of governmental entities.
  • Experienced Project Team – We have an established culture that is focused on listening to and responding quickly - empowering teams to meet and exceed customer expectations.

  • Value: Lowering Your Cost - Field crews utilize the latest technologies - such as CREWtek™ - to deploy the right equipment and people to do the job as you expect.
  • Safety is of the upmost importance to Terracare Associates-TCA and a core value.  Our goal is for every employee, and all of our customers to go home safe and healthy every day.
  • Trained and Solid Structure and Process - We deploy highly trained staff that is flexible and efficient in planning, managing and maintaining your public works or infrastructure departments and projects. Every employee attends mandatory equipment and safety training and most have multiple certifications.
  • Honor Schedules and Budgets - We understand the importance of keeping projects on schedule and on budget while maintaining the level of service you need.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible to the environment and our community is part of our core values; we strive to be good corporate citizens every day and on every job.
