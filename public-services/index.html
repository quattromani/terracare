Public Landscape and Public Infrastructure Maintenance

Terracare Associates (TCA) is at the forefront of public outdoor maintenance including, municipal landscape maintenance, public works contractor and public-private partnerships (P3). TCA specializes in city landscape management and public infrastructure maintenance services. At TCA, we strive for superior customer service. We are a trusted partner utilizing dedicated and professional staff, state-of-the-art technologies, and reliable equipment to get the job done on time and within budget. Effective communication to our customers is of upmost importance to ensure absolute customer confidence. Experience matters, as our leadership team has an average over 20 years working in outdoor maintenance, landscape and construction industries. Our highly trained staff is flexible and efficient in managing and maintaining physical infrastructure, parks, streetscapes, highways and roadways to successfully serve cities, counties, public highway authorities and metro districts.

Terracare Associates can provide you with:
  • Strong Governmental Experience - Public-private partnership (P3) is a way for governments to collaborate with the private sector to share the risks and rewards involved in the construction and maintenance of public infrastructure projects.  TCA leadership and management have extensive experience working with P3 projects and a number of governmental entities. We are familiar with managing public expectations and incorporating effective communication processes for a wide variety of stakeholders.
  • Experienced Project Team – We have an established culture that is focused on listening to and responding quickly to the needs of the customer. Our entire business is built around the concept of “team”. However TCA offers a single-source contact for each client for consistency and to build a trusted relationship.
  • Quality Assurance Program - Our Project Manager, Assistant Project Manager, Foremen and upper management will conduct regular quality control audits. We will then generate any necessary work orders for repair or maintenance solutions in our work order management system and complete the necessary corrections in timely and efficient manner.
  • Value: Lowering Your Cost Through Efficiency - Terracare Associates’ field crews utilize CREWtek™, a handheld device that lets our staff manage projects remotely, handle billing, manage equipment and schedule crews efficiently and effectively. All Terracare Associates vehicles are equipped with the latest GPS equipment. This technology allows us to track time spent at your site and provides a method for efficient routing, especially in emergencies, so we can be at your site at the right time.
  • Safety is a core value of our company. Every employee attends safety training before working in the field with our company. We offer incentives to those that continue to use excellent safety practices. Each week, the staff reviews job safety record and proactively mitigates risk through open and honest discussion of improvement.
  • Trained and Solid Structure and Process: - TCA understands that having a highly trained staff that is able to develop a flexible and efficient plan to manage the maintenance of your public landscape and infrastructure is key to successfully serving you as a long term client. Every employee attends mandatory equipment and safety training and most have multiple certifications. With 400 employees nationwide, our employees are not siloed. Cross-training our staff to be proficient in multiple service lines helps us to provide the most efficient and cost effective service possible to our customers.
  • Honor Schedules and Budgets - We understand the importance of keeping projects on schedule and on budget while maintaining the level of service you desire. Our team members are able to respond quickly when called upon for your outdoor maintenance needs, and we work closely with multi-governmental agencies.
  • Good Stewards of our Communities – Being responsible to the environment and our citizens is part of our everyday work. Sustainability & Responsibility Policy
