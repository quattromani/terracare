Terracare Associates is the preferred public works/public infrastructure maintenance provider on a number of highway and toll authorities in Colorado and growing. Projects include the E-470 Public Highway Authority, Northwest Parkway Public Highway Authority and the I-25 Corridor through metro-Denver.

Terracare Associates understands that having a highly trained staff that is able to develop a flexible and efficient plan to manage the maintenance of your physical infrastructure is key to successfully serving you and your clients. The TCA teams for our public infrastructure maintenance projects are led by experienced, highly trained, professional Project Managers who will have responsibility for the entire project.

With 400 employees nationwide, Terracare Associates has the experience, knowledge and ability to provide quality public infrastructure maintenance services to our clients. Our teams are experienced, dedicated and professional.

Current contracted highway maintenance projects include routine maintenance, repair, and renovation and services include:
  • Native mowing
  • Pothole repair – asphalt & concrete
  • Sweeping
  • Sign installation
  • Trash pick up
  • Weed control
  • Snow and ice control
  • Emergency response
  • Courtesy patrol
  • Crack seal
  • Striping
  • Traffic control
  • Concrete – sidewalk
  • Drains
  • Shoulder maintenance
  • Lighting
  • Guardrails
  • Fence repair
  • Washout repair
