Dean Murphy
President

Dean Murphy has over 30 years experience in the landscape maintenance, public infrastructure and construction industries. He has managed many multimillion dollar projects in several states including construction, installation and renovation of complex developments. After graduation Dean started and led construction business for ten years. Later he became Vice President at American Civil Construction working in multiple states with both private and public entities and eventually became President of Terracare Associates 2010. Dean Murphy is a recognized leader in the industry with knowledge, reputation of working with both government and private organizations. Through his leadership and agile management style he successfully nurtured the thriving growth the company enjoys today. Always striving for high quality service and customer satisfaction, he puts great focus on hiring and mentoring a superior management team. Dean believes his team members are the most important asset of the company and has built a “family” culture emphasizing leadership, integrity, values and respect for the environment. He is passionate about the communities where TCA operates and encourages volunteering and sponsorships supporting the outdoors.
